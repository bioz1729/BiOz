Menu

  • Chicken Curry:
    • A flavorful and spicy chicken gravy made with a blend of South Indian spices and coconut milk.
  • Mutton Curry:
    • Tender mutton pieces cooked in a rich, aromatic gravy with spices and herbs.
  • Fish Curry:
    • Fish simmered in a tangy and spicy coconut-based gravy, often flavored with tamarind.
  • Mushroom Curry:
    • Succulent Mushroom cooked in a spicy and coconutty gravy.
  • Egg Curry:
    • A mild and creamy chicken curry with coconut milk, often served with appam or idiyappam (string hoppers).
  • Andhra Chicken Curry:
    • A spicy and tangy chicken curry with a distinct Andhra flavor, usually made with fiery red chilies.
  • Chettinad Chicken Curry:
    • A rich and aromatic chicken curry from the Chettinad region known for its use of bold spices like black pepper and star anise.
